About Us

Hi, my name is Bekki, I am head chef and owner of B’Central. I was born and raised in East London with my Mum, Nan and grandad that’s where my love for cooking started. 

I later went to New York, attended culinary school to learn the best ways to enhance my skills and love for the arts. During my internship I met amazing people who loved food but wanted a personal experience different from the norm leading to the start of B’Central. I not only pride myself in serving amazing food but also giving each customer an awesome experience. 

The feeling of being in the kitchen cooking with my Nan and having all my friends and family around, eating good food was a wonderful feeling. I aim to not only satisfy your stomach but make your evening that much more fulfilling.
